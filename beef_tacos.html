Beef Tacos

Ingredients

  • 2 tsp paprika
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp oregano
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 1/2 tsp cornstarch
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 400g lean beef mince
  • 2 carrots, shredded
  • 1 zucchini, shredded
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 60ml water
  • Per Serve:
  • 1/2 tbsp Greek yoghurt
  • 2 taco shells
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato, diced
  • 2 tbsp grated cheese

Instructions

  1. 1. In a large saucepan, over medium heat, lightly spray some olive oil and add the onion, spices, and beef mince to the pan.
  2. 2. Cook the beef mince completely.
  3. 3. Once cooked, add the cornstarch and water, and allow to simmer until the water forms a gravy-like consistency.
  4. 4. Layer your tacos with the beef mince, top with Greek yoghurt, diced tomato, lettuce, and cheese.
